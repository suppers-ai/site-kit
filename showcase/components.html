Sticky top bar with three named slots: brand, nav, actions. At narrow viewports the slots stack vertically; no hamburger menu in v2.

KindNameNotes
slotbrandLogo or wordmark, usually inside an <a>.
slotnavA <nav> with 2–5 links.
slotactionsOne or two CTA links (sign-in, GitHub, …).
partcontainerThe outer <header>.
partbrandWrapper around the brand slot.
partnavWrapper around the nav slot.
partactionsWrapper around the actions slot.

<sa-hero>

Splash block parameterized by attributes. Optional media slot promotes the layout to two columns on wide viewports. CTA renders only when both cta-href and cta-label are set.
